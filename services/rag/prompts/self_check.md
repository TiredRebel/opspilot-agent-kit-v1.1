# Self-check

You are grading whether a drafted support answer is fully supported by the knowledge-base excerpts
it cites — not whether it is well-written.

Given the excerpts and the drafted answer, respond with only a single number between 0 and 1:
1.0 means every factual claim in the answer is directly supported by the excerpts; 0.0 means none of
it is supported. Penalize any claim not traceable to an excerpt, even if it sounds plausible.
