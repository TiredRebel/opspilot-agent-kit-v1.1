# Billing & Plans

This document covers the full Acme Cloud Suite plan and pricing breakdown, how annual billing
works, and how to upgrade or downgrade your plan.

## Plan and pricing breakdown

Acme Cloud Suite has four plans, all billed per user:

| Plan | Monthly price | User limit | Storage | Support SLA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0/user/month | Up to 3 users | 1GB | 48-hour email response |
| Starter | $9/user/month | Up to 20 users | 20GB | 48-hour email response |
| Pro | $19/user/month | Unlimited users | 200GB | 24-hour priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom pricing via sales | Unlimited users | Unlimited storage | 4-hour response, dedicated account manager, phone support |

Free is intended for very small teams (up to 3 users) or for evaluating the product. Starter
and Pro scale up in user count and storage. Enterprise pricing is arranged directly with our
sales team and includes unlimited storage.

## Annual billing

Starter and Pro plans can be billed annually instead of monthly. With annual billing, you pay
for 10 months and get 12 months of service — an effective discount of about 17% compared to
paying month to month. Annual billing is charged as a single upfront payment at the start of the
billing year. Free does not require billing, and Enterprise billing terms are set in your sales
contract.

## Invoices and payment methods

Invoices are generated automatically at the start of each billing cycle (monthly or annual,
depending on your billing frequency) and are available for download from Billing Settings.
Acme Cloud Suite accepts major credit cards for Starter and Pro; Enterprise customers may also
arrange invoiced payment terms through their account manager.

## How to upgrade your plan

To upgrade (for example, from Starter to Pro, or from Free to Starter):

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Plan**.
2. Select the new plan and confirm.
3. The upgrade takes effect immediately, and the cost difference for the remainder of your
   current billing cycle is **prorated** and charged right away. You do not need to wait for
   your next billing date to get access to the new plan's features, users, and storage.

## How to downgrade your plan

To downgrade (for example, from Pro to Starter):

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Plan**.
2. Select the new, lower plan and confirm.
3. The downgrade is scheduled to take effect at the **start of your next billing cycle**. You
   keep your current plan's features until then. Downgrades are **not refunded** for the
   remaining time on your current cycle — you keep access to what you already paid for, but
   there is no partial refund for switching down early.

If you need to reduce your user count below your current plan's limit, remove users from your
workspace before or after the downgrade takes effect, as needed.

## Switching between monthly and annual billing

You can switch from monthly to annual billing (or vice versa) at any time from
**Settings > Billing**. Switching to annual billing applies the 10-months-for-12 discount
starting with your next payment. See the Refund & Cancellation Policy document for how the
14-day money-back guarantee interacts with your first purchase, and for what happens to your
data if you cancel entirely.
